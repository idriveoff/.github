## 🧠 Overview

**iDrive** is a versatile and reliable cloud backup solution that provides both personal and business users with a secure way to store and protect their important data. With its advanced cloud storage features, iDrive ensures that your files, photos, videos, and system data are backed up in real-time, giving you peace of mind against data loss.

Whether you need to back up your photos, documents, or entire systems, iDrive offers an efficient solution. It supports automatic, continuous backups, allowing you to focus on your tasks while the system ensures your data is safely backed up to the cloud. With powerful encryption, you can rest assured that your files are protected with the highest level of security.

iDrive also provides a unique feature that allows you to sync and share files across multiple devices, making it easier to collaborate with teams or access your files from anywhere. Additionally, its interface is user-friendly and easy to navigate, ensuring an optimal backup experience for users of all levels.
